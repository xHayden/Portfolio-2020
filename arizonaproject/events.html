Events

Arizona Event Information:

Here are the events I will be doing over my 7-day trip to Arizona.

Some are paid adventures. However over half of them are free.

Phoenix:

Morning Hot Air Balloon Ride

A hot air balloon view of the Sonoran desert in Arizona. Event starts at 5 AM. The trip lasts for 3 hours 30 minutes, however the actual flight lasts about an hour. You get to see the expansive landscape of Arizona in its full glory.

$179 per person

Apache Trail/Canyon Lake Steamboat Cruise

Full day tour of the Apache Trail and Canyon Lake. You visit the ghost town of Goldfield, as well as take a steamboat cruise on Canyon Lake.

$150 per person

Camelback Mountain

This mountain offers a variety of activities including rock climbing, hiking, and hot air balloon rides. It is a challenging hiking location that offers amazing scenery.

Hiking is free


Sedona:

Antelope Canyon Tour and Horseshoe Bend

You are picked up from your hotel and driven through Oak Creek Canyon towards Antelope Canyon. After visiting a Cameron trading post, you hike Horseshoe Bend before returning back to your Sedona Hotel.

$220 per person

Devil’s Bridge Trail

Devil's bridge is a rock “bridge” overhanging a drop. Easy hiking trail with beautiful view. Nice relaxed trip compared to the other, more strenuous hikes planned for other days.

Hiking is free


Tucson:

Madera Canyon

A cool location with brown rolling hills topped with greenery.

Hiking is free

Sabino Canyon

Located in the Catalina Mountains and part of the Coronado National Forest. Hiking, swimming, biking, and more is offered at this location.

Hiking is free
