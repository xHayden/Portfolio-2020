Recommendations

Recommendations:

These are my recommendations for the locations and transport I've listed.

GreenTree Inn Sedona

This hotel was the best in every way. It had an amazing view, easy access, and featured a copious amount of complementary items. Plus, it was extremely affordable.

$100 per night

Antelope Canyon Tour and Horseshoe Bend

This full-day tour was one of the most memorable parts of my trip. The guides were nice, and took us through many inquisitive areas. I was even able to buy many souvenirs from the native people.

$220 per person

Camelback Mounyain

This was the most challenging part of the trip, but it was well worth it. The view was stunning, and offered a great challenge. It was free, but I would say it was a better experience than some of the paid trips on this list.

Hiking is free
